    A Recipe for Creativity

    This 5 special creative ingredients make up a recipe that's good for your soul, so start dreaming, indulge in your passions, be proud of your uniqeness and find the courage to turn your ideas into action.

  • Ingredients:

    • 300g of Immaturity
    • 260g of Foolishness
    • 1/4 cup of Emotion
    • 1/2 cup of Bravery
    • 1 teaspoon of Unconventionality

Ordered list

    Method:

  1. Preheat your brain to 106 degrees celcius.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the immaturity and foolishness.
  3. Gradually add the emotion and combine with the bravery, spead half if this mixture into the bottom of the preheated brain and add a thin layer of unconventionality.
  4. Pour this mixture to the right side of your brain and bake for 10 - 20 minutes.
  5. Let it rest for 5 minutes
Pedro Gaspar
